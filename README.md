# Super-store-Analytics
--To analyze sales performance across products,segments, category and sub-category and yearly trends.
**##Tools used**
--Power BI
--Tableau
--Excel**
**###Key Insights****
--Sales has been increased over the years,showed the consistent growth over the year-over-year growth.
--Categoey and sub-category wise sales to show which segment is contributing the most to get teh higher sales.
--Top performing products to see which products customers are loving the most,to identify the customer preferences.

--These KPI's together gives a detailed overview of sales performance in different segments
**##Outcomes***
Helps management to identify top products,profitable segments, and optimize discount startegies,promotional offers.
